Model Evaluation

The evaluation of the proposed ResNet-101 model is based on precision, recall, F1-score, and accuracy. These metrics provide a comprehensive understanding of the model's performance in classifying 32 different types of retinal diseases using the EyeNet dataset.

Model Accuracy Precision Recall F1-Score
ResNet-101 98.75% 99% 99% 99.5%

The table above demonstrates that the ResNet-101 model achieves exceptional performance, with nearly perfect precision, recall, and F1-score. The model's high accuracy of 98.75% on the validation set indicates its robustness in classifying retinal diseases, making it a reliable tool for early diagnosis and treatment planning. These results highlight the effectiveness of the proposed deep learning approach in improving the accuracy and efficiency of retinal disease classification compared to traditional diagnostic methods.
